What is RootRel

One ecosystem for every part of the trip.

RootRel brings together the tools travelers usually piece together from five different apps — planning, community, safety, and rewards — into a single connected experience.

AI Planning

An itinerary engine that adapts to your pace, budget, and interests as you go.

Travel Communities

Join groups of travelers heading the same direction, before you even land.

Emergency Assistance

One-tap SOS with location sharing, built for the moments plans fall apart.

Social Travel

Share a live feed of your trip and discover what other travelers are seeing right now.

Gamification & Rewards

Complete challenges as you travel and unlock rewards from partner brands.

Hidden Gems

Surface the places locals recommend, not just the top ten lists.

Anywhere You Go

From snowfields to jungle canopies.

RootRel is built for the full range of a traveler's map — the AI planner, community feed, and SOS tools work the same whether you're above the treeline or deep in the rainforest.
